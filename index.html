Water-Borne Diseases Awareness

Cholera

Cause: Contaminated water & food

Symptoms: Severe watery diarrhea, vomiting, leg cramps

Prevention: Drink only boiled or treated water, wash hands often.

Typhoid

Cause: Salmonella bacteria in water

Symptoms: Prolonged fever, weakness, stomach pain, headache

Prevention: Vaccination, avoid raw foods, safe water.

Dysentery

Cause: Bacterial or amoebic infection

Symptoms: Diarrhea containing blood/mucus, fever, cramps

Prevention: Good sanitation, pure water, hand hygiene.

Hepatitis A

Cause: Virus in contaminated water/food

Symptoms: Fatigue, nausea, abdominal pain, jaundice (yellow skin)

Prevention: Vaccine, peel raw fruits/veg, safe water.

General Prevention Tips

Boil drinking water for 1-3 minutes.
Wash hands with soap before eating.
Keep water containers covered and clean.
